How the Skin Works

Epidermis

The outer layer protects you from germs and keeps moisture in. It constantly sheds dead cells and makes new ones.

Dermis

The middle layer gives skin strength and flexibility. It contains collagen, elastin, fibroblasts, hair follicles, sweat glands, and blood vessels.

Hypodermis

The deepest layer stores fat, cushions the body, and helps regulate temperature.
